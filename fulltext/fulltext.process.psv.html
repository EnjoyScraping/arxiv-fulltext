fulltext.process.psv module¶

Utilities to convert text extracted from a PDF (usually from pdf2txt) to clean format in which only ascii characters are left and sentences are separated by new lines (PSV). This utility was converted from arXiv::Overlap::TidyText in Perl, retrieved 2012-05-06 [CVS: v1.3 2011/08/15 14:55:33 arxiv Exp].

This should be replaced by a new fulltext mechanism which provides an alternative text extraction method available more broadly than just in docsim.
